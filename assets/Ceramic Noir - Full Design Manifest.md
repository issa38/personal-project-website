## Brand Philosophy
Ceramic Noir is built on the intersection of quantitative logic and architectural elegance. It uses deep, obsidian-inspired neutrals and subtle textures to create a grounded, professional environment for finance, strategy, and AI-focused narratives.

## Core Visual Principles
- **Tactile Depth:** Subtle textures and soft gradients that mimic the surface of high-end stoneware.
- **Quantitative Clarity:** High-contrast typography and structured modular grids to ensure complex data remains legible and impactful.
- **Architectural Movement:** Interaction design focused on precision, stability, and fluid, purposeful transitions.

## Color Palette

### Surfaces
- **Surface (Deep Obsidian):** `#131313` - Primary background foundation.
- **Surface Dim:** `#1c1b1b` - Subtle depth, container backgrounds.
- **Surface Bright:** `#3a3939` - Elevated surfaces and highlights.
- **Surface Container Lowest:** `#0e0e0e` - Deepest background levels.
- **Surface Container Low:** `#181717` - Card and section backdrops.

### Accents & Text
- **Primary (Matte Sandstone):** `#A38A7E` - Primary CTA, active states, and focus highlights.
- **On-Primary:** `#131313` - Text on primary accent surfaces.
- **On-Surface (Stone Grey):** `#E0D9CD` - Primary body text and high-contrast labels.
- **On-Surface Muted:** `#7d7d7d` - Secondary text, metadata, and inactive states.
- **Border/Divider:** `#2d2d2d` - Subtle structural definition.

## Typography

### Families
- **Display & Headings:** `Noto Serif` (or equivalent high-contrast Serif).
- **Body & Technical UI:** `Inter` or `Geist` (Modern, clean Sans-Serif).

### Styles
- **Hero Display:** 64px+, Light (300), -0.02em tracking.
- **Section Heading:** 32px, Regular (400), uppercase with 0.05em tracking.
- **Body Text:** 16px, Regular (400), 1.6 line-height.
- **Data Label:** 12px, Bold (700), uppercase, 0.1em tracking.

## Layout & Components

### Grid System
- **Desktop:** 12-column modular grid.
- **Margins:** 48px to 80px (generous whitespace for "breathing room").
- **Gutter:** 24px to 32px.

### Visual Grammar
- **Corner Radius:** `ROUND_FOUR` (4px to 8px) - Maintains a precise, architectural feel.
- **Borders:** `1px` solid `#2d2d2d` for container definition.
- **Shadows:** Deep, soft elevations (`shadow-[0_32px_64px_-12px_rgba(0,0,0,0.8)]`) used sparingly for depth.
- **Imagery:** High-contrast, moody photography with soft, directional lighting and architectural/organic subjects.

## Interaction Patterns
- **Magnetic Buttons:** Primary CTAs exhibit a subtle magnetic pull towards the cursor.
- **Staggered Reveals:** Content sections should animate in with a slight vertical delay (20px translate-y).
- **Tactile Feedback:** Active states should utilize a subtle scale-down (`scale-95`) to provide immediate feedback.
- **Smooth Transitions:** Page transitions should be fluid and purposeful, reinforcing the sense of precision.
